How You Can Help

In order to deliver programs and services that are so essential to our clients, Blue Hills Child and Family Centre depends on financial support from government, corporations, foundations, community organizations and individuals. Blue Hills appreciates the support of people like you to help Blue hills continue offering innovative and effective programs to families in need.

We gratefully welcome donations of any size. Charitable donation receipts will be issued for all gifts over $20.00.

Your gift can be designated to Blue Hills or you can request that your gift be used where help is needed most.

You can donate to Blue Hills through
 • monthly gifts
• one time gifts
• in memoriam and in honorarium
